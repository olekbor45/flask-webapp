Overview of Incoterms

When exporting or importing, it is crucial to choose an appropriate Incoterm. Although Incoterms may seem complex, selecting the correct one is a foundational decision that dictates the responsibilities, costs, and risks divided between the seller and the buyer.

Category E

The buyer bears responsibility (EXW). The seller must ensure the goods arrive at the agreed delivery address.

Category F

The seller arranges delivery. The buyer assumes responsibility afterward.

Category C

The seller pays until goods arrive. Risk shifts to buyer during onward transport.

Category D

All obligations are tied to the destination agreed upon by the parties.
