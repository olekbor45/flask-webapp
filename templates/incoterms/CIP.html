CIP

CARRIAGE AND INSURANCE PAID TO

Carriage and Insurance Paid To (CIP) is an Incoterm where the seller delivers the goods to a carrier, pays for the transport to a named destination, and also pays for insurance covering the buyer’s risk during transit.

Key Points:

1.Seller covers transport and minimum insurance to the named destination.

2.Risk transfers to buyer when goods are handed over to the first carrier.

3.Seller handles export clearance.

4.Buyer is responsible for import duties and additional insurance if needed.

CIP is similar to CPT but adds the seller’s obligation to insure the goods.
